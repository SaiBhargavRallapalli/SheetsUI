Privacy Policy for SheetsUI

Last updated: February 2025

Overview

SheetsUI ("the App") allows you to view and manage Google Sheets and Forms on your Android device. This policy describes how we handle your information.

Data We Collect and Use

Google Account Data

  • The App uses your Google account to access Google Drive, Sheets, and Forms.
  • We request access only to the data needed for the App's features (Drive, Spreadsheets, Forms).
  • Your credentials and tokens are processed by Google's services and stored securely on your device.

Data on Your Device

  • The App may store cached spreadsheet data locally to support offline use.
  • You can clear this data by clearing the App's storage in your device settings.

No Selling of Data

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for advertising or other commercial purposes.

Third-Party Services

  • Google APIs: The App uses Google Drive, Sheets, and Forms APIs. Their use of data is governed by Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Firebase Authentication: We use Firebase for authentication. See Firebase Privacy.

Security

We follow Android security practices and use HTTPS for network requests. Sensitive data is stored using Android's secure storage mechanisms.

Your Rights

You can revoke the App's access at any time via your Google Account permissions.

Contact

For questions about this policy, contact us via your preferred support channel.
